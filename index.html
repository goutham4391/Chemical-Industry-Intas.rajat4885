Intas Pharmaceuticals Ltd.

Intas is one of the leading multinational pharmaceutical formulation development, manufacturing and marketing companies in the world. Today, Intas is present in more than 85 countries worldwide and is growing at ~22% CAGR. Around 70% of its revenues come from the international markets, particularly the highly regulated markets of EU and US. At Intas, every day and everyone works to create a world of good health, happiness and hope.
